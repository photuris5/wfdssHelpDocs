WFDSS NextGen 2025 Release Update

January 2025

WFDSS NextGen development is in the final stage ahead of becoming the system of record. The next 3 months will be exciting as we wrap up feature development for the first release (1.0). Over the next 3 months and WFMRDA will be engaging in robust testing, supporting training and course content development, as well as inviting users to participate in application testing and provide feedback, all in preparation of the full transition to WFDSS NextGen on March 26th.

The current operational version of WFDSS will be maintained in its current state of Operations and Maintenance (O&M) with no Development Modernization or Enhancements (DME) taking place. Patching and necessary technical upgrades and changes necessary to comply with CIO policy and hosting environment requirements will continue until the Next Generation WFDSS is ready for production use and to prepare for its archiving.

A reminder WFDSS NextGen is available for user testing in the "User Acceptance Testing (UAT" environment.  All users that have requested accounts have been granted access for UAT with roles that match their role in WFDSS Classic. Please note that you can't mess anything up by utilizing WFDSS UAT so test away.

We are working hard to get the Training environment stable and available for users as quickly as possible. As soon as Training (EDU) is available, we will be notifying users and granting system access.

WARNING: This is a development environment with changes/updates occurring daily.  Not all functionality is available at this time and many of the issues you may encounter are known and in process.  Thank you in advance for your patience as we work out the kinks. 

We want to hear from you.

  • Submit feedback related to appearance and functionality at WFDSS NextGen Feedback.

  • Before submitting your feedback, please double check your issue hasn't already been captured in the Feedback Inventory, which will be updated periodically.

We look forward to hearing your feedback!

Interested in Participating in User Groups?

  • Submit feedback related to appearance and functionality in a facilitated user feedback session.

  • Presentations and user feedback from previous User Group sessions are available in the User Feedback Topic.

  • Sign up if you would like to be included in User Group mailings.

 

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get access to the WFDSS NextGen UAT interface?

At this time, only those users with existing Geographic Area Editor (GAE) roles in WFDSS Classic have been granted roles to start exploring in the Testing environment. As more functionality becomes available and GAE feedback is incorporated, users desiring Editor or Analyst roles will be invited to request access.

What should I do after my WFDSS NextGen UAT GAE role has been approved?

  • Set your Geographic Area and Agency pair in the GAE tab of your profile. When the system is ready for further role testing, GAEs will be responsible and provided with instructions on how to approve Editor and Analyst roles, much like in the WFDSS Classic workflow.

  • This is a Testing environment similar to WFDSS Classic Training – actions taken in the UAT will not have any consequences to the decisions related to actual fires. It (probably) can’t be broken.

  • Take note of functionality that is not intuitive or language that is confusing and provide feedback to the Next Gen team.

What types of feedback should users submit related to the UAT environment?

  • This environment is in active development with the potential for daily changes, including full system resets. Not all functionality is available at this time and many of the issues you may encounter are known and in process. 

  • We want your feedback related to appearance and functionality at WFDSS NextGen Feedback . Before submitting your feedback, please double check your issue hasn't already been captured in the inventory, which will be updated periodically.
