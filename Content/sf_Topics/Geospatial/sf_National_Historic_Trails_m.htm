National Historic Trails

The data displayed in (Undefined variable: General.ProductName) are intended for strategic use only. Always verify the accuracy of the data with the creators of the data and local knowledge.

Description

Data are aggregated from authoritative sources based on web services displayed at https://nps.maps.arcgis.com/apps/webappviewer/index.html?id=d89951079a374f28ab4a3b9fc41025dd . Data are loaded here for use in interagency wildland fire applications. Always consult authoritative data sources or agency officials for conditions on the ground.

Aggregation is performed on an annual basis. Data last updated August 2023.

During processing, the Ala Kahakai National Historic Trail was converted from a polygon to a line feature class so that it could be included in this line feature service.
