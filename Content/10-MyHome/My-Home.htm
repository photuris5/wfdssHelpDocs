My Home

 

The My Home tab provides users with quick access to user-relevant information.

Find out more about the My Home tab by visiting the User Group session #2 information page or YouTube presentation.

My Active Incidents

Active incidents are those on which you are a collaborator.  The most recently edited or accessed incident will be on top, but the table is sortable by any of the headers.  The items in the table are hyperlinked to the greatest extent possible.  For example, clicking on the lead collaborator or incident name will navigate directly to that user’s profile or the Incident Dashboard, respectively.  As development progresses within the incident, the data that displays under the various headers of the table will be linked to those modules of the incident record.  If a user would like to view a full list of their Active Incidents or download a .cvs file, click the See More button .

My Incident Watchlist

Incidents you have added to My Incident Watchlist are those that you have activated the "watch" icon. A user does not need to be a collaborator to add an incident to their Watchlist. The most recently edited or accessed incident will be on top, but the table is sortable by any of the headers.  The items in the table are hyperlinked to the greatest extent possible.  For example, clicking on the lead collaborator or incident name will navigate directly to that user’s profile or the Incident Dashboard, respectively.  As development progresses the data that displays under the various headers of the table will be linked to those modules of the incident record.  If a user would like to view a full list of their Active Incidents or download a .cvs file, click the See More button.

Links

Users can access Significant Weather and Fire Behavior Information and Other Intelligence by following the hyperlinks to authoritative sources at the upper right of the My Home screen.
