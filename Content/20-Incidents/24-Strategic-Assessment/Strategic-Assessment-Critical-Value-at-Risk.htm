Strategic Assessment Critical Value at Risk

In order to incorporate the Incident Strategic Alignment Process (ISAP), the option to mark a Strategic Assessment as a Critical Value at Risk has been added to WFDSS NextGen. In order to be marked as a Critical Value at Risk (CVAR) a Strategic Assessment must be included in the decision. A CVAR is composed of four fields, 3 user-populated and one calculated. CVAR fields are based on the ISAP Strategic Risk Assessment Terminology & Definitions v7 and are as follows:

  • Impact Severity - This field is rating of what the severity of impacts would be should the fire or suppression actions affect that value. Impact Severity is classified as one of the following:

    • Catastrophic - Value is destroyed or unusable for months, will permanently affect the natural environment (irreversible), threat to survival of fauna, flora, cultural, or heritage resources.

    • Critical - Value is destroyed or unusable or restricted for weeks, impact can be repaired with appropriate resources, threat to damage fauna, flora, cultural, or heritage resources.

    • Moderate - Some portion is unusable or restricted but can be restored within an acceptable timeframe, short-term impact that is able to be addressed through existing processes, impairment of fauna, flora, cultural, or heritage resources.

    • Negligible - Minor damage or only temporarily unavailable or restricted, little to no action needed for mitigations post event, insignificant impact to cultural/heritage resources, potentially beneficial response from fauna/flora resources.

  • Probability of Impact - This field is a rating based on the likelihood of an event occurring, using one of the following ratings:

    • Almost Certain - Expected to occur or is happening now.

    • Likely - Will occur frequently, very realistic to occur.

    • Possible - Could occur under specific conditions & some of those conditions are currently evidenced.

    • Unlikely - Remotely possible but not probable, could occur but deemed unfeasible.

    • Rare - Improbable but has occurred in the past.

  • Risk Rating - is a calculated field based on Impact Severity and Probability of Impact and the CVAR Risk Assessment Matrix below.

  • At Risk Because of - is a classification field for the CVAR used to differentiate the source of the threat. There are two options:

    • Potential Fire Behavior

    • Fire Management Actions

In WFDSS NextGen, CVARs are limited to 10 Strategic Assessments for fires less than 10,000 acres and to 25 for fires more than 10,000 acres.
