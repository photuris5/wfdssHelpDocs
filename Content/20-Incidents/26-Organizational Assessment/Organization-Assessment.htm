Organization Assessment (OA)

(Part C, PMS 236)

NOTE: Per the 2023 PMS 236 the Part C Organization Assessment is intended for those incidents that either going to be managed for an extended period of time whether through extended attack or being managed to meet resource objectives.

Relative Risk must be assessed before completing the OA, as its inputs feed directly into the OA. Each of these must be completed before a pending decision can be published.

The PMS 236 Part C - Organization Assessment (OA), is composed of 7 elements:

  • C1 - Potential Fire Duration

  • C2 - Incident Strategies (Course of Action), and the Relative Risk Rating (PMS 236 - Part B)

  • C3 - Functional Concerns

  • C4 - Objective Concerns

  • C5 - External Influences

  • C6 - Ownership Concerns

The cumulative rating of the elements determines the recommended organization, the user can then select their choice for the 'Unit Recommended Organization'.

If either the 'Unit Recommended Organization' or the 'Organization Assessment Results' indicate a T1/CIMT or a T2/CIMT then a Part D - Functional Complexity is required.

As the complexity of an incident changes over time, the required IMT type may also change. However, the strategic decision should be reviewed in its entirety to ensure that any changes in organizational structure do not necessitate modifications to the decision and vice versa.
